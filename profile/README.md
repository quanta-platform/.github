## What is Quanta?

Quanta is a next-generation e-commerce platform purpose-built for **Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSMEs)**. We believe every growing business deserves enterprise-grade infrastructure — without the enterprise price tag.

<br>

## ✨ Why Quanta?

🏪 **Multi-Store Ready**
Run multiple storefronts from a single platform. Each store gets its own isolated environment — separate products, pricing, orders, and team members.

📦 **Real-Time Inventory**
Track stock across warehouses and suppliers in real time. Never oversell, never miss a restock.

🔄 **Automated Order Workflows**
From placement to fulfillment, every step of the order lifecycle is automated and auditable.

💬 **Integrated Messaging**
Reach your customers where they are — SMS, WhatsApp, and email notifications built right in.

💰 **Built for Budget**
Designed from the ground up to minimize infrastructure costs. Enterprise features at startup pricing.

🔒 **Secure by Design**
Strict data isolation between stores, role-based access control, and industry-standard authentication.
